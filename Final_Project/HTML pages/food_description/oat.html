OAT

Oat is a species of the grain cereal and the seeds are harvested from the Avena grasses. They then undergo the process of cleaning and hulling. The machine removes the unwanted materials from the oats. Next, the screening machinery removes unwanted oats base on its size using air to remove loose oats. Then, the outer shell is removed from the inner kernel by machines that fling the oats against a ring to knock the hull off. For the conditioning process, steam increases to give the groats a nutty flavor. Then the flaking system produces flakes which are then dired on a bed dryer before being packaged.
