COCONUT OIL

Through the dry process, coconut oil can be extracted from the dried copra of the coconut. Copra is created by extracting the meat from the coconut and drying it with fire, sunlight, or kiln. The copra is then pressed or dissolved with solvents to create a mash along with coconut oil. As for the wet process, coconut milk is extracted from the raw coconut. Then, this goes through boiling to produce oil. Modern techniques can use centrifuges and pre-treatments to remove certain fatty acids to reduce the possibility of spoiling.
