PEANUT OIL

Peanuts is a type of legume developed from the peanut plant. Although the peanut plant flower grows above ground, the seeds or peanuts grow underground. Peanut oil can be extracted by a peanut oil machine. The machine first shells the peanuts and with the cleaning sieve, it removes the impurities. Then the peanuts are cooked or roasted and then through a physical pressing process, the oil is squeezed out. Later, the machine filters out any other impurities to maximize quality of the remaining oil.
