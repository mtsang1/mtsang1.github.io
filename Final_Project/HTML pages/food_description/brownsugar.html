BROWN SUGAR

Brown sugar can contain anywhere between 3.5%-10% of molasses based on its total weight or volume. Itâ€™s often produced by adding molasses derived from sugarcane to refined white sugar crystals. This type of preparation produces coarse brown sugar. The white sugar that is used in this mixture can come from beet or cane.
